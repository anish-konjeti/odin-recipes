Lasagna

Description

I suppose since I have recipes for Skillet Lasagna, Lasagna Soup, Lasagna Roll Ups, and Taco Lasagna, we are long overdue for me to share my easy, classic lasagna recipe.

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and lots of mozzarella cheese.

This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

Ingredients

  • Butter for greasing
  • 2 1/2 cups Lady Caterina's Ragu
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese
  • 3 hard cooked eggs finely chopped
  • 2 cups of bechamel
  • 2 cups of finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • A pound of store-bought pasta sheets

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, eggs, bechamel, and 1 cup of the Parmigiano-Reggiano. Set aside.
  3. Spread 1/2 cup of the ragu in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Layer 3 pasta sheets over the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the pasta sheets. Top with 1/3 of the remaining ragu and sprinkle with 1/3 of the remaining Parmigiano-Reggiano. Repeat layering two more times, ending with a layer of ragu and Parmigiano-Reggiano on top.
  4. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until bubbly and golden brown on top. Let rest for at least 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
