Chicken Burger

Description

A chicken burger the whole family enjoys. Natasha is my eldest daughter, and her eyes light up when I announce we're having these for dinner. The onion can be finely chopped, but with three kids, we prefer grating it so as to hide it. I usually serve these with mashed potatoes and Caesar salad for a very comforting meal.

This top-rated chicken burger recipe is a refreshing change from traditional beef burgers.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound extra lean-round ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup italian-seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 small onion, finely grated
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the grill or skillet.
  2. Combine ground chicken, onion, ¼ cup bread crumbs, egg, and garlic in a bowl; season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Moisten hands; shape chicken mixture into 4 flat, oval-shaped patties.
  4. Spread remaining ¼ cup bread crumbs in a shallow dish; dip patties in bread crumbs to coat.
  5. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Add patties; cook until golden and well browned on the bottoms, 5 to 6 minutes.
  7. Flip patties; cook until other sides are browned, 3 to 4 minutes more. Serve and enjoy!
